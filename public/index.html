About me

I’m a graduate student at Northeastern University, MA, pursuing Master of Science in Information Systems. I pursued my bachelors in Electrical Engineering and worked in Infosys, India, for three years as Senior Systems Engineer where I have designed technical specs, developed and maintained OpenEdge product used in financial & insurance services and delivered customization for various clients. I have gained expertise in writing data extraction scripts and creating field mappings for transformation in relational databases. At Northeastern University, I have taken courses towards Big Data Analytics, Data Science and Software Engineering.

I’m interested in finding meaningful insights from large datasets and have taken up the initiative to do my class projects on datasets like Enron, Yelp, 100k movielens, Wikipedia, NOOA’s weather dataset and the like. I took Web Development and Design to explore the vast field of visualization and have built Web Applications using Spring Security, Hibernate, MySql and worked on technologies like Git, Travis CI, Maven, REST, Node.js, AngularJS and D3.js. I am enthusiastic about plethora of libraries and tools out there to build impressive front-end designs using complex data models behind the scenes for mesmerizing user-experience. As an Intern at Harvard Medical School, MA, I led the data migration of their legacy Specimen Storage System having gigs of data from SYBASE to existing application, BioSTOR, in SQL Server from design, development to deploying in production. I was responsible for data profiling, developing workflows, mappings, rules specifications, designing and developing migration application scripts, user interface and also dealing with high priority requests during which I worked on technologies like SQL Server Management Studio, Sybase ASA, Informatica Analyst, MicroStrategy, and SVN. This was all a part of Scrum/Agile Methodology to provide data-driven solutions. Currently I am developing a website with MEAN stack and building Resful webservices to perform CRUD operation and also integrating the view with D3.js with some interesting visualizations using directives as elements in AngularJS.
